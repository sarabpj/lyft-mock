Rides in Minutes

Wherever you're headed, count on Lyft for rides in minutes. The Lyft app matches you with local drivers at the tap of a button. Just request and go.

How Lyft Works

1.Request

You've got options. Request Lyft, Lyft Plus, Lyft Premier, or Lyft Line.

2.Ride

Get picked up by the best. Our reliable drivers will get you where you need to go.

3.Pay

When the ride ends, just pay and rate your driver through your phone.

Lyft Line

Lyft Line is a shared ride. It matches you with other going the same way, so you can split the cost. Price is fixed upfront and always less than original Lyft. Available in select U.S. cities.
